# Hi, I'm Artur

I built e-commerce brands for 10 years. Scaled a DTC portfolio from $1M to 8 figures, ran teams of 60 people, got very good at making numbers go up. Somewhere along the way I started wondering if any of it would matter in 5 years.

Turns out, most of it won't. The job titles, the org charts, the "what do you do?" answer at parties — AI is about to make all of that irrelevant. I don't think that's a bad thing. I think it's overdue.

So now I build AI that actually lives with you. Not a chat window you visit when you need something. A system that holds your context across months, runs its own research, manages your infrastructure, and gets better at helping you the longer it exists. Closer to a second brain than an assistant.

First-gen immigrant. Belarus → California. Self-taught in everything that matters.

## What I'm Building

**[Arcimun](https://arcimun.com)** — My own personal AI platform. Multiple agents with persistent memory, running locally, handling research and operations around the clock. Not a ChatGPT wrapper. Actual infrastructure.

**[Ember](https://github.com/arcimun/ember)** — Voice to text for macOS. Press a key, speak, text appears in any app. No cloud, no subscription. Open source.

**[Проект Раскрытие](https://t.me/disclosure_x)** — Russian-language research channel covering UAP disclosure, consciousness, and ancient civilizations. Facts and primary sources, not hype.
